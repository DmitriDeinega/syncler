**Audit Log**
Denied calls/signature failures/hash mismatches are logged with Timber tag `PLUGIN_AUDIT` and appended to:

```text
context.noBackupFilesDir/pluginhost_audit.log
```

Typically:

```text
/data/user/0/app.syncler.android/no_backup/pluginhost_audit.log
```
