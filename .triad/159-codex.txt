Findings first:

- **FIX:** Inner cap math is too loose. With max channel 64 and 32-char `uuid.hex`, compact frame overhead is about 148 bytes, not 64. Current cap `49088` base64s to `65452`; outer frame becomes about `65600`, over `65536`. Even short channel can exceed by a few bytes. Use a computed post-wrap size check, or reserve worst-case overhead and base64 padding correctly.
- **FIX / 5th thing:** Android live client is still built with `baseUrl = "https://syncler.local"` in `PluginLoader.android` (`PluginLoader.kt` around line 248). The rest of the app appears to use `BuildConfig.SERVER_BASE_URL`. Unless `syncler.local` is intentional production config, this means the inbound path is not actually usable end-to-end from the standard Android loader.
