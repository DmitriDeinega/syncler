=== GUT TAKE ===
The idea is strong, but the product is currently three products wearing one coat: human-in-the-loop agent control, structured service-to-human workflows, and personal handoff/storage. Prior Claude was right about the core wedge, right about scope being the main risk, and right that declarative-first beats plugin-first for V1. I think prior Claude may have been slightly too accepting of the Alpaca/KYC framing as near-term product direction; that path drags in trust, compliance, identity, encryption, liability, and enterprise sales before the product has earned any of that weight. The most important unpressed question is: what exact painful workflow will the user personally run through this app every day for 30 days, even if no third party ever integrates?
