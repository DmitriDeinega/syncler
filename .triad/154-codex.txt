docs/integration-guide.md:766 says every WS frame has `{type, id, channel, payload}` shape. Shipped `server/app/routers/live.py` does not wrap sender push frames that way: `push_live` serializes `body.envelope` directly and publishes that raw JSON to device sockets (`envelope_json` at live.py:222, `publish_ephemeral(..., envelope_json)` at live.py:240-241). This is integration-breaking for live push frame consumers.
