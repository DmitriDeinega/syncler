### Line-level Concerns

1.  **`Dismiss` handler is a no-op:**
    In `InboxViewModel.kt`, the `Dismiss` event triggers `repository.refresh()`. However, `InboxRepository.refresh()` only appends new messages to the local list and never prunes or updates existing ones. Furthermore, the server-side `inbox_for_device` (in `server/app/services/messages.py`) does not join with `DeliveryStatus` to filter out dismissed messages. 
    **Impact:** A message dismissed on Device A will remain visible on Device B even after Device B receives the SSE event and "refreshes". This appears to be a pre-existing platform limitation that Phase 2 inherits.

2.  **SSE Reconnection:**
    `EventStreamManager.kt` sets `current = null` on failure but does not attempt to reconnect. While `ON_RESUME` restarts the stream, a user staying in the app during a momentary network drop will lose real-time updates until they background/foreground the app. Consider a basic retry loop for future hardening.

3.  **Plan Contradiction (`Last-Event-ID`):**
    The Phase 2 plan's "Tests" section mandates that "SSE reconnect uses `Last-Event-ID`," but the "Scope Recap" explicitly defers this. The current implementation correctly follows the deferral (leaving it as a future hardening).
