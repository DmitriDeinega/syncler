### Consultation 49: Final Consensus & Roadmap Ratification

I have reviewed the arguments from both sides and the proposed roadmap. Below is the final synthesis and resolution.

---

### Disagreement 1 — Migrating local pairings
**Decision: Hybrid "Push with Migration Flag"**
I concede to the UX argument: "nothing works on new devices" is a worse failure than "resurrected pairings." 

*   **Minimum-correct implementation:** On the first unlocked launch after M7 sync lands, the client scans the local `PairedSenderStore`. Any pairing missing from the synced state is pushed to the M7 blob. 
*   **Safeguard:** These entries are marked with `source: "migration"` in their metadata. The Inbox UI will display a one-time banner: *"We've synced pairings from your other devices. [Review] [Dismiss]"*. Clicking "Review" opens a filtered view of the "migrated" pairings for easy bulk-cleanup.

### Disagreement 2 — "Mute" Sync
**Decision: Synced Primary with Local Override**
The "bedside tablet" case is valid, but the "mute once, fix everywhere" case for spam is the 90% use case.

*   **Minimum-correct implementation:**
    *   **Synced State:** Stores `muted_senders: Set<SenderId>`.
    *   **Local State:** Stores `mute_overrides: Map<SenderId, Boolean>`.
    *   **Logic:** `isMuted = local_override ?? synced_default`.
*   **UI:** The toggle in the settings sheet primarily targets the synced state. A "Mute only on this device" option is tucked under an "Advanced" or "More" chevron in the same sheet.
