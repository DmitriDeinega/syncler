### Specific Concerns

#### 1. Lexical Timestamp Sorting
In `SyncedPairedSenderStore.kt` and `InboxRepository.kt`, timestamps are compared as strings. As fixed in `StateMerger.kt`, this can lead to incorrect ordering when some timestamps have milliseconds and others don't.

*   **`SyncedPairedSenderStore.kt:144`**: `.sortedBy { it.firstPairedAt }`
*   **`SyncedPairedSenderStore.kt:181`**: `.maxByOrNull { it.firstPairedAt }`
*   **`InboxRepository.kt:254`**: `candidates.sortedByDescending { it.firstPairedAt }`

**Recommended Fix:** Move the `compareTimestamps` logic from `StateMerger` to a common utility or use `Instant.parse(it.firstPairedAt)` in these collectors.

#### 2. Re-pair Tombstone Timestamp
In `SyncedPairedSenderStore.add`, the `now` timestamp for tombstones is generated outside the `mutateAndPush` block. While not a bug (the call is serialized), it's more idiomatic to generate it closer to the mutation or use the `Clock` consistently to ensure deterministic tests.
